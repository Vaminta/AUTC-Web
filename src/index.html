Aberystwyth University Tennis Club

Welcome to AUTC! We're looking for new members, whether you're a tennis pro or have never picked up a racket, or simply feel like trying a new sport.

About Us

Hello and welcome to Aberystwyth University Tennis Club 2021/22. Our club welcomes all abilities from no experience and social players all the way up to representing the University in BUCS and beyond.

What's on

Sessions will be run by different committee members. Sessions have a variety of activities throughout them, such as games, drills and matches. These sessions are available to all members regardless of experience and is a great way to improve skills and practise your tennis. Equipment is provided by the club free of charge to all members.

Touch Tennis

This new development for the tennis club is included in your tennis membership, however if you wish to only play touch tennis then there is a separate membership available. With smaller courts, nets, rackets and lower bouncing balls as well as an indoor space Touch Tennis is the perfect way to play regardless of experience and fortunately is not affected by weather. Touch Tennis is not just for beginners, everyone is welcome, and we have a great set of games for sessions. All equipment is provided by the club free of charge. The timing of these sessions are TBC.

Socials

Socials are run by our social secretaries and these are a great way for all our members to socialise outside the confines of the tennis courts and meet our many non-tennis playing members. There are various events that occur at socials and these will be announced at a later date. Watch this space.
